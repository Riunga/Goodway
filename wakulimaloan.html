Wakulima Loan

Agricultural Loan

Our Wakulima loan products support farmers with affordable financing options tailored to agricultural needs.

Loan amounts and terms depend on the type of farming and expected yields.

Collateral requirements vary based on loan size and crop type.

Eligibility
  • Must be a registered farmer with a valid license
  • Must have operated a Goodway Sacco account for at least 6 months
  • Must provide a viable farm plan
Loan Term

Up to 24 months

Benefits
  • Flexible repayment schedules aligned with harvest cycles
  • Competitive interest rates for agricultural loans
  • Access to agricultural advisory services
  • Quick loan processing and disbursement
