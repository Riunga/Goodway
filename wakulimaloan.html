Specialized agricultural financing to support farmers and boost agricultural productivity

Agricultural Financial Solution
Our Wakulima loan products are specifically designed to support farmers and agricultural entrepreneurs with affordable financing options tailored to their unique needs. We understand the seasonal nature of farming and provide flexible solutions that align with harvest cycles and agricultural production schedules.
Loan Features
  • Loan amounts from KSh 20,000 to KSh 5,000,000
  • Flexible repayment terms up to 24 months
  • Special agricultural interest rates starting from 11% per annum
  • Repayment schedules aligned with harvest seasons
  • Agricultural advisory and extension services included
