Our Story

The Beginning

Our journey began in 2007 as Tescom Society in Kirinyaga County, Kenya. We started with a clear mission: to empower private school employees and teachers with reliable savings and credit services.

Growth & Expansion

As trust grew, we expanded to serve farmers, business owners, and institutions. We evolved beyond traditional banking, handling salary processing and building a solid financial foundation. In 2012, we also grew to introduce Tescom Insurance Agency, where we help and provide insurance services for our customers, expanding our offerings to include comprehensive insurance solutions.

Rebranding to GoodWay

In 2014, our growth culminated in rebranding to GoodWay Sacco Society Limited, reflecting our broader mission and evolution into a comprehensive financial partner for diverse membership.
