About Goodway Sacco Society

Goodway Sacco Society is dedicated to providing reliable and innovative financial services to our members. We strive to empower our community through savings, loans, and investment opportunities tailored to your needs.

Our History

Our solid background in banking. Get to know about our company. Registered in 2007 as Tescom Society, in 2014 it rebranded to GoodWay Sacco Society Limited. It was started to offer savings and credit services to private schools employees, the business community, and civil servants.
