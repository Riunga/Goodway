Our Journey

From humble beginnings to becoming a trusted financial partner

Our solid background in banking spans over 17 years of dedicated service. What started as Tescom Society in 2007 has evolved into Goodway Sacco Society Limited - a testament to our commitment to growth and member satisfaction.

We were founded with a clear mission: to offer comprehensive savings and credit services to private schools employees, the business community, and civil servants. Today, we continue to honor that mission while expanding our reach and services.

2007

Foundation

Started as Tescom Society, providing essential savings and credit services to our community.

2014

Transformation

Rebranded to Goodway Sacco Society Limited to better reflect our growing membership and expanded services.

2024

Continued Growth

Serving 2,500+ members with innovative financial solutions and maintaining SASRA accreditation.
