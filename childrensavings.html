Future-Focused Banking
Our Children's Savings Accounts are specially designed to help parents and guardians build a secure financial future for their children. With competitive interest rates, educational benefits, and flexible contribution options, we're here to support you in creating lasting financial security for the next generation.
Account Benefits
  • Higher interest rates than regular savings
  • No monthly maintenance fees
  • Parental control and monitoring
  • Educational resources and financial literacy
  • Gift contributions from family members
  • Automatic transfers and standing orders
  • Special birthday and holiday bonuses
  • Transition to youth account at age 18
