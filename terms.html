Understanding your rights and responsibilities as a member of Goodway Sacco Society

Introduction

Welcome to Goodway Sacco Society. These terms and conditions govern your membership and use of our financial services. By becoming a member or using our services, you agree to be bound by these terms.

Goodway Sacco Society is a licensed Savings and Credit Cooperative Organization (SACCO) regulated by the SASRA (Sacco Societies Regulatory Authority) of Kenya.

Membership Eligibility and Requirements

Eligibility Criteria

  • You must be at least 18 years old
  • Valid identification document (National ID, Passport)
  • Proof of residence in Kenya
  • Clean criminal record
  • Minimum share purchase as stipulated

Share Requirements

Each member is required to purchase a minimum of one share, currently valued at KSh 100. Additional shares may be purchased to increase your voting rights and dividend entitlements.

Membership Fees

A one-time membership fee of KSh 500 is required upon registration. This fee is non-refundable and covers administrative costs.

Loan Terms and Conditions

Loan Eligibility

  • Active membership for at least 3 months
  • Regular savings contributions
  • Good repayment history
  • Sufficient collateral or guarantors

Loan Types and Terms

Personal Loans

Up to KSh 500,000 with repayment periods of 6-24 months. Interest rate: 12%-15% per annum.

Business Loans

Up to KSh 2,000,000 with repayment periods of 12-36 months. Interest rate: 10%-16% per annum.

Wakulima Loans

Agricultural financing with harvest-aligned repayments. Interest rate: 10-12% per annum.

Loan Processing Fees

A processing fee of 1-2% of the loan amount applies, payable upon approval.

Default and Penalties

Late payments attract a penalty of 5% per month on outstanding amounts. Persistent default may result in legal action and membership suspension.

Savings and Deposit Terms

Mandatory Savings

Members are required to maintain a minimum monthly savings contribution of KSh 1,000.

Interest Rates

  • Fixed Deposits: 5%-10% per annum (depending on term)
  • Children's Savings: 4% per annum

Withdrawal Terms

Regular savings can be withdrawn at anytime with a convinience of using Digital Banking or in-person transactions. Fixed deposits have lock-in periods and early withdrawal penalties apply.

General Terms and Conditions

Data Privacy

We are committed to protecting your personal information. Your data is used solely for membership and service provision purposes and is not shared with third parties without your consent.

Service Availability

Amendments

These terms may be amended by the Board of Directors. Members will be notified of any changes via email, SMS, or posted notices at our offices.

Governing Law

These terms are governed by the laws of Kenya and subject to the jurisdiction of Kenyan courts.
